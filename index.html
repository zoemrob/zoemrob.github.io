A little about me...

Self-taught. Ambitious. Developed in Portland, OR.

I love to code. Programming was something I had always thought about getting into, but imagined it as being too difficult. College (and the money involved) was a luxury not a right. I recall reading an article and a statistic about how by 2020 there will be a projected two million job openings in Software Development with only 800,000 C.S. majors graduating.

The opportunity was too tempting to resist!

So I started plugging away. I determined if I was to be successful as a self-taught programmer I needed to devote as much - or more - time to programming as if I were going to college for it. For the next 12 months, I went at least 10-15 hrs a week.
